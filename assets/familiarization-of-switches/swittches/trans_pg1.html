Introduction


There are 2 main types of Transistors.

They are:
1.Bipolar junction transistor
2.Field-effect transistor



A Bipolar junction transistor (BJT) is the type of transistor and three-terminal semiconductor device, which has two p-n junctions. They are mainly used as amplifiers or current controlled devices in electronic circuits.


BJT is further divided into:

1.NPN

2.PNP


A Field-effect transistor (FET) is a type of transistor commonly used for weak-signal amplification (for example, for amplifying wireless signals). The device can amplify analog or digital signals.


FET is further divided into:

1.JFET

2.MOSFET
