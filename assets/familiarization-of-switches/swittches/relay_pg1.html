Relays


The numbers of Switches inside a relay is called the poles of the relay.The number of circuits being controlled per pole is called the throw of a relay.


Relays are classified based on poles and throws as

  • Single Pole Single Throw Relays (SPST)
  • Single Pole Double Throw Relays (SPDT)
  • Double Pole Single Throw Relays (DPST)
  • Double Pole Double Throw Relays (DPDT)
